Description

Colgate Renewal toothpaste for early gum disease neutralizes plaque bacteria and helps to reverse early gum disease damage. This means less bleeding gums and inflammation, so you’ve got gums that are healthy and revitalized!

Plus, this daily whitening fluoride toothpaste gently whitens teeth to remove surface stains, so your smile is renewed and glowing again!

Our translucent, light teal toothpaste gel has a Cool Mint flavor and freshens breath, so you can feel confident and ready for the day.

This fluoride toothpaste has technology that is backed by clinical research. It is also a vegan toothpaste, sugar-free, gluten-free, and enamel safe for daily use. Happy brushing!

User Instructions:
Adults and children 12 years of age and older: apply at least a 1-inch strip of the product onto a soft bristled toothbrush. Brush teeth thoroughly for at least 1 minute twice a day (morning and evening) or as recommended by a dentist. Make sure to brush all sensitive areas of the teeth. Do not swallow.
*Children under 12 years of age: consult a dentist.
